04 — Key Takeaways

What the Data Says

⛰
July is King
July 2024 saw 1.31M vehicles through the tunnel — the highest single month, driven by summer mountain tourism and outdoor recreation.
❄
Ski Season Surge
January–March accounts for roughly 26% of annual tunnel traffic, with February weekends seeing the most intense congestion of the year.
⏰
Leave Before 6:30 AM
On a Saturday ski day, leaving Denver at 5:30 vs. 7:30 AM can save you nearly an hour of driving. The difference is dramatic.
🚘
Sunday Afternoon Gridlock
Eastbound I-70 congestion on Sundays typically starts around 1 PM, peaks at 3–5 PM, and doesn't clear until after 7 PM. Average delay: 60+ minutes.
📈
Steady Growth
Tunnel traffic has grown from about 11.5M vehicles (2022) to over 12.9M (2024), a 12% increase in just two years.
🚋
Transit Is Expanding
CDOT's Snowstang bus and the Mountain Express Lane (tolled shoulder) are attempts to ease pressure, though capacity remains limited.
