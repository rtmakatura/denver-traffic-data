08 — Key Takeaways

What the Data Tells Us

⚠️
I-70 & Peoria Is #1
103 crashes in 2024. A complex merge where highway speeds meet local traffic.
📱
Distraction Dominates
40% of all Denver crashes involve distracted driving.
🚶
Pedestrians at Risk
590 pedestrian crashes in 2024, with 38% at mid-block crossings.
📅
Tuesday Surprise
Tuesday is consistently Denver's most crash-prone day.
🏙️
Aurora's Problem
Nearly half of Colorado's 50 most dangerous intersections are in Aurora.
🚨
85% Preventable
CDOT estimates 85% of Denver traffic crashes are preventable.
